### 8.3 Methodology

**Data Collection**: Four independent workflows scanned sources within their respective scan windows (WF1/WF3/WF4: 24h, WF2: 48h) anchored to a common T0 timestamp. Each workflow operates with complete data isolation -- no cross-workflow data sharing during collection, analysis, or report generation.

**Classification**: All signals classified using STEEPs 6-category framework. WF3 and WF4 additionally apply FSSF 8-type classification, Three Horizons tagging, and Tipping Point detection.

**Ranking**: Unified pSST (probability-weighted Signal Significance & Timing) score calculated per-workflow, then normalized to a common 0-100 scale for cross-workflow ranking. Top 20 signals selected by unified pSST with representation consideration.

**Integration**: Single-agent integration (report-merger fallback mode) applied to merge four independently complete reports. Cross-workflow analysis identifies reinforcements, contradictions, academic early signals, and exclusive signals per workflow.

**Quality Assurance**: Each workflow report validated with profile-specific `validate_report.py` checks (15-20 structural checks per profile). Integrated report validated with `integrated_en` profile. VEV (Verify-Execute-Verify) protocol applied at each phase transition.
