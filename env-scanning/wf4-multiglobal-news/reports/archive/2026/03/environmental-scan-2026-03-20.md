### 8.4 FSSF Classification Methodology

The FSSF (Future Signal Scanning Framework) 8-type classification is based on standard futures research methodology. Each signal is classified according to the following criteria:

- **Weak Signal**: Very early-stage change indicator with high uncertainty but potential to seed future transformation
- **Wild Card**: Low probability event with extreme impact if it occurs -- discontinuous and unpredictable
- **Discontinuity**: Event signaling abrupt break from existing regime, potentially catalyzing systemic transformation
- **Driver**: Major force driving change, providing directional momentum to other signals
- **Emerging Issue**: Issue rapidly rising on public agenda but not yet mainstream
- **Precursor Event**: Specific event that foreshadows larger change
- **Trend**: Change with identifiable direction and momentum, supported by data
- **Megatrend**: Large-scale, long-duration trend with broad societal impact representing structural change
